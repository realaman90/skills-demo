## Key Frameworks

1. **CBBE Pyramid** (Keller): Salience → Performance + Imagery → Judgments + Feelings → Resonance
2. **Brand Knowledge Model**: Awareness (recall + recognition) + Image (associations: favourability × strength × uniqueness)
3. **Feldwick's Three Strands**: Brand strength (loyalty) | Brand value (financial) | Brand description (image)
4. **CSR Equity Building** (Hoeffler & Keller): 6 mechanisms, conditions for effectiveness, cause-washing risk
5. **Dilution Risk Matrix** (Keller): Extension fit × success/failure → impact on parent equity
6. **Financial Valuation Methods**: Royalty relief, economic use, Interbrand methodology
