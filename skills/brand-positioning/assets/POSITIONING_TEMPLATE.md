## Positioning Checklist
- [ ] Target market defined behaviourally — not just demographically
- [ ] Frame of reference chosen deliberately (not defaulted to)
- [ ] Category POPs all met — brand has category credibility
- [ ] PODs tested against Desirable / Deliverable / Differentiating criteria
- [ ] Reasons to Believe identified for each POD
- [ ] POP-POD conflicts identified and resolved
- [ ] Positioning statement written in standard format
- [ ] Brand mantra meets Keller's three criteria
- [ ] Positioning stress-tested: are claimed differences actually perceptible to buyers?
